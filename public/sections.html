How I get ready for school.

The morning routine of a fourth year UCSD student.

I think that people have interesting morning routines so I will describe my personal experience getting ready for school on a typical day in this article.

  • Good Days
  • Not So Good Days
Because people naturally have better days than others, I can explain what I do (or don't do) on those days. Included in this article is my morning routine for good days and not-so-good days.

This section is about my good days.

When do I get up?

I get up as soon as I hear my alarm.

How do I decide on what to wear?

Check the weather and think about event's on my calendar and dress for the occasion.

What do I eat?
My favorite cereal recipe.

Pour Reese's Puffs into a large bowl. Then drown it in milk. Spoon optional.

This section is about my not-so-good days.

When do I get up?

I hit snooze and get up at the last possible minute.

How do I decide on what to wear?

I wear anything that feels comfortable and that doesn't smell.

What do I eat?

I grab a granola bar and hurry to catch the bus because I spent too long in bed.
