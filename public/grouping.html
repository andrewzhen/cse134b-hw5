Cats may not be "man's best friend", but they are definitely a joy to have around. A few types of cats include:

  • Persian cat
  • Russian Blue cat
  • British Shorthair cat

However, my top 5 favorite cats in reverse order are:

  1. Siamese cat
  2. Bengal cat
  3. Munchkin cat
  4. Main Coon cat
  5. American Shorthair cat

Here are some facts about cats:

Breed Characteristics
Siamese cats are vocal and also like to communicate with their bodies.
Maine Coons are athletic and will generally refuse to sit in your lap.
Early Life
Kittens who are exposed to other cats early on tend to be more social in general while those that are isolated tend to remain this way into adulthood.
Good nutrition correlates with friendliness in kittens.
